- Growth Green: `#2E9E5B`

- Charcoal: `#33423D`
- Light Gray: `#F4F6F5`

The Uthaan symbol shows a school rising from an open book, representing trust, learning, achievement, and the mission of helping Pakistani schools rise from registers and spreadsheets into one organized future.
