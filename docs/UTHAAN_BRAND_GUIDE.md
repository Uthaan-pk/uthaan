# Uthaan Brand Guide

## Brand Meaning

Uthaan means rising, uplift, and progress.

The Uthaan symbol combines a rising school roof, an open book, upward movement, and a small flag.

- The roof represents the school as a trusted institution.
- The open book represents learning, records, and academic growth.
- The upward form represents Uthaan itself: lifting schools toward a more organized, modern future.
- The small flag represents achievement, direction, and a school reaching a higher standard.

Uthaan exists to help Pakistani schools move upward from registers, spreadsheets, and scattered systems into one organized future.

## Usage Guidance

- Use the primary logo on light backgrounds.
- Use the reversed logo on dark green backgrounds.
- Use the icon for compact spaces such as sidebar, favicon, mobile app shell, and small cards.
- Keep Uthaan's visual tone premium, calm, trustworthy, education-focused, and modern.
- Do not stretch, recolor, distort, or overcrowd the logo.
- Do not use the logo near claims about features that are not live.
